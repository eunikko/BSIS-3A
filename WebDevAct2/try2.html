NBA2K

NBA2K is a series of basketball sports simulation video games developed by Visual Concepts and released annually since 1999. The premise of the series is to emulate the sport of basketball, more specifically the National Basketball Association. The latest version in the franchise is NBA 2K24, which features updated graphics, new gameplay mechanics, and a range of modes, including MyCareer, MyTeam, and The City.
